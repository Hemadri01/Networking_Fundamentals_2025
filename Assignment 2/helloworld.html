Course Information

Welcome to Networking Fundamentals.

Course Objectives:

  • An introduction to the concepts, principles, and practice of computer communication networks with examples from existing architectures, protocols, and standards.
  • Topics include; Internetworking Fundamentals, Protocols, Routing, Switching, congestion control

Course Duration: 12 weeks

Schedule:

  • Friday 11:30 AM - 1:05 PM

References

  • Computer Networking: A Top-Down Approach, Jim Kurose & Keith Ross, Addison-Wesley, 2016
  • Computer Networks: A Systems Approach (5th edition), by Larry Peterson & Bruce Davie, 2011
  • Richard Stevens: TCP/IP Illustrated, Volume 1: The Protocols, 1994
  • Andrew Tanenbaum: Computer Networks, 2022
  • Douglas E. Comer Computer Networks and Internets, 2018
  • D. Bertsekas & R. Gallager, Data Networks, 1992
